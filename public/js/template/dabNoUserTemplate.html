Scan your card to get started

This is a demonstration of using Facebook Device Auth to integrate Facebook Platform with your existing loyalty or customer ID system.

The technology is agnostic to the identification system used - it just needs to be a number unique to a user. For example, you could use a customer loyalty card number, a primary key from your user database or a user registration email address.

Let's assume you have a store card for NQP, and there's a barcode scanner attached to this computer. You'd be asked to scan the card. Hit the button below to simulate scanning your card, or type into the input box first to reuse an existing code.
